Hello there, and welcome to my digital portfolio!

I am a Front End Developer with the experience and tools to work across design and tech teams. Whether turning user-driven wireframes into code, working directly with designers to create intuitive web applications, or gathering business requirements and documentation for new technologies, I am an adaptable design enthusiast with development expertise.

My primary focus is front-end technologies (HTML5/CSS/JS), while the front-end frameworks I typically use are AngularJs (1.5), React, and Vue.js. I understand the versatility of different programming languages and how their applications can best be suited for specific project goals. I am also familiar with backend tech such as Node.js, MySQL and MongoDB.

I continue my professional growth daily in web development, and spend a good amount of free time fine-tuning my skills with photo editing and vector drawing software. The Adobe Creative Suite is my favorite digital playground. Beyond that, I love cooking, sports with friends at the park, botany (at home and at the office), snowboarding, and pursuing a motivated and active lifestyle!
